CARE Malawi

Project completed: May 2015

Tools used: WordPress CMS, HTML/CSS, PHP, JavaScript/jQuery

The “Raising the Score” website was one of my first tastes of working under a client with a deadline, and with a diverse team of coders, designers, and videographers.

Through the JOMC 584 class, my classmates and I worked together to built a website for CARE, an NGO dedicated to fighting global poverty. They hired our team of journalists to go to Malawi for ten days and document their maternal healthcare program, and then build a website including the media.

Although I was unable to attend the trip, I was able to hone my web development skills through this project, from building page templates, to troubleshooting PHP issues, to figuring out how to get those tricky interactive graphics to be responsive.
