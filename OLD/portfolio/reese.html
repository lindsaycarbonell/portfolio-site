Reese News Lab

Internship from: May - Aug 2015

Tools used: reporting, writing, entrepreneurship, Adobe Illustrator, Google API, HTML/CSS, JavaScript, videography/video editing

Reese News Lab is a sort of pre-start-up factory for the journalism industry. During the summer, I and my other ten colleagues broke into three teams. Our job was to tackle one of three prompts through the lens of journalistic intent: to help people make better, more informed decisions about their lives. After many hurtles and problem solving, our team came up with a product called Blue Wagon. Blue Wagon simplifies the inundation of information a homebuyer is faced with in the real estate market through a map and video product that I prototyped during our last week of the internship.
